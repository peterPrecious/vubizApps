[[The Employee Training Centre is where you can enroll members of your team to view selected Learning Themes.]]  [[Once you select one or more Themes, you can then enroll your employees.]]  [[If you wish to disable this service, simply deselect all Themes.]]

[[Note: To offer courses to employees in both English and French you must select ALL THEMES. If the portal is offered to employees in only one language you may select the individual themes to be displayed.]]

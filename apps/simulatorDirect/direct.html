Create an Ecommerce Transaction from Scratch

Fill in the form values (with two programs from your catalogue) and Submit. This will post your transaction values to the Ecommerce Engine. Use "Validate" to ensure you are using the right values. Using "Commit" will actually post the transaction - if successful. Note: the two field names at left are what the Engine expects (top) and what the Web Service expects (bottom)

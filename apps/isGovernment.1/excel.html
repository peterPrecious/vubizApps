This creates an Excel report of all active learners in the selected units and the Programs they have accessed during the selected dates. Tap Generate Spreadsheet and the number of rows required will be calculated and displayed. If you'd like to continue, then reports containing 5000 or less rows will be generated in formatted Excel while reports with more than 5000 rows will be generated in unformatted CSV.
